Dog Facts

  1. The fastest dog breed is the Greyhound, which can reach 45 miles per hour!
  2. Three dog breeds survived the Titanic, Pekingese, Pomeranian, and Newfoundland!
  3. The French Poodle isn't actually French, it's German!
  4. A Shar-Pei actually has a purple and not red tongue!
  5. A Kerry Blue Terrier is actually born as black, but as it ages up until around two, it becomes blue!
